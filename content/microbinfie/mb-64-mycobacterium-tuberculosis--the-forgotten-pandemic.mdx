# The Forgotten Pandemic: Mycobacterium Tuberculosis

We continue our series examining specific microbial species in depth. In this installment, we discuss the often overlooked pandemic caused by *Mycobacterium tuberculosis*. Our distinguished guests include:

- **Dr. Suzie Hingley-Wilson**  
  *Lecturer in Bacteriology at the University of Surrey*

- **Dr. Dany Beste**  
  *Senior Lecturer in Microbial Metabolism at the University of Surrey*

- **Dr. Conor Meehan**  
  *Assistant Professor in Molecular Microbiology at the University of Bradford*

Stay tuned as we delve deeper into the characteristics, challenges, and ongoing research concerning this significant microbial species.

The speakers on the Microbial Bioinformatics podcast include Dr. Lee Katz, Dr. Nabil Ali Khan, and Dr. Andrew Page as hosts. The guest speakers featured are Dr. Susie Hingley-Wilson, Dr. Danny Best, and Dr. Connor Meehan.
