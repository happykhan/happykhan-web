## Interview with Dr. Ozan Gundogdu

**Guest:** Dr. Ozan Gundogdu  
**Topic:** Insights into the Foodborne Pathogen *Campylobacter*  
**Hosted by:** [Institution Name]

### Overview

Today, we are excited to host Dr. Ozan Gundogdu to discuss *Campylobacter*, a notable foodborne pathogen. This conversation will cover significant advancements over the past 20 years, driven by genomics since the publication of the first reference genome in 1999.

### About Dr. Ozan Gundogdu

- **Position:** Leader of the Foodborne Enteric Pathogen Group
- **Affiliation:** London School of Hygiene & Tropical Medicine
- **Expertise:** Physiology and pathogenesis of *Campylobacter* and related microorganisms like *Listeria* and *Vibrio*.
- **Educational Background:** Molecular Biology and Computer Science.
- **PhD:** Completed at LSHTM in 2011.

Dr. Gundogdu's work emphasizes the importance of understanding the physiology and pathogenesis of enteric pathogens, focusing on how genomic advancements have reshaped the field.

### Further Information

For more insight into Dr. Ozan Gundogdu's research and publications, please visit his [profile at LSHTM](http://www.lshtm.ac.uk/aboutus/people/gundogdu.ozan).
