# Bacterial Taxonomy Discussion

In our latest discussion, we explore the evolving field of bacterial taxonomy. This session focuses on:

- New methodologies for naming numerous unnamed and uncultured bacterial organisms.
- The controversial renaming of bacterial phyla.

### Featured Guests

- **Professor Phil Hugenholtz**
- **Professor Iain Sutcliffe**
- **Professor Mark Pallen**

These experts share their insights and the latest research on how the scientific community approaches the taxonomy of bacteria. They discuss the challenges and implications of introducing new naming conventions and the ongoing debate surrounding the renaming of phyla.
