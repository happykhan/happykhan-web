## Microbial Bioinformatics

This chapter or section, titled "Phylogenetics with the Arborists Part 2," focuses on the field of microbial bioinformatics, particularly in the context of phylogenetics. Below is an outline or summary inspired by the title and context:

1. **Overview of Phylogenetics in Microbiology**
   - Introduction to phylogenetic principles.

2. **Tools and Techniques**

### Important Terms

- ***Phylogenetics***: The study of evolutionary relationships among biological entities.
- ***Microbial Bioinformatics***: The application of bioinformatics techniques to study microorganisms.
- ***Horizontal Gene Transfer* (HGT)**: The transfer of genetic material between organisms in a manner other than traditional reproduction.
