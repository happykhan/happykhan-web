# Discussion on SARS-CoV-2 Genomics with Peter van Heusden

We are joined by **Peter van Heusden** to discuss the latest developments in SARS-CoV-2 genomics, with a focus on tools and resources. Peter also shares insights into the challenges of building and sharing data in large-scale sequencing endeavors.

## Tools and Resources

- **Nextalign**
  - [Nextalign on GitHub](https://github.com/nextstrain/nextclade/releases)
  
- **COG Mutation Explorer**
  - [COG Mutation Explorer](http://sars2.cvr.gla.ac.uk/cog-uk/)

- **Grinch**
  - [Grinch (CoV-Lineages)](https://cov-lineages.org)

- **New US Tool for Variants**
  - [CDC Variants Information](https://www.cdc.gov/coronavirus/2019-ncov/transmission/variant-cases.html)
  - [CDC Global Variant Map](https://www.cdc.gov/coronavirus/2019-ncov/cases-updates/variant-surveillance/global-variant-map.html)
