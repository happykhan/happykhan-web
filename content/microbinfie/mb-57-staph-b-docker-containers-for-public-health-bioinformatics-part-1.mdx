### Key Points

#### 1. Career Transitions in Bioinformatics
- Both guests started on pre-med tracks before discovering a passion for research
- Transitioned from traditional wet lab work to computational biology
- Valued quick feedback loops and scalability of computational approaches

#### 2. Public Health Bioinformatics Fellowships
- Participated in APHL/CDC bioinformatics fellowship program
- Worked at state public health laboratories
- Developed skills in genomic sequencing and analysis

#### 3. StaPH-B Community and Collaboration
- Introduced to StaPH-B community during early career stages
- Emphasize importance of open-source development and collaborative spirit
- Established Slack channel for communication among bioinformatics scientists

### Take-Home Messages
- Bioinformatics offers diverse career paths beyond traditional medical tracks
- Public health fellowships provide critical training and networking opportunities
- Collaborative communities are essential for advancing scientific research
