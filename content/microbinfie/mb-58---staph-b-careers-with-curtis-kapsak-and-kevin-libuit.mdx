# Exploring Curtis's and Kevin's Careers

In this article, we take a closer look at the careers of scientists Curtis and Kevin. Below are relevant resources that provide insight into their fields of expertise and the opportunities available in their professional areas.

## Relevant Links

- [Skype a Scientist](https://www.skypeascientist.com/): A platform connecting scientists with classrooms for virtual educational sessions.
  
- [APHL Bioinformatics Fellowship](https://www.aphl.org/fellowships/Pages/Bioinformatics.aspx): A fellowship program by the Association of Public Health Laboratories focused on bioinformatics, offering training and professional development for early-career scientists.
  
- [JMU Genomics](https://www.jmu.edu/genomics/index.shtml): The Genomics program at James Madison University, which offers resources and educational opportunities in genetics and genomics.
  
- [JMU Biology](https://www.jmu.edu/biology): The Biology Department at James Madison University, providing information on their programs, research, and faculty contributions.

These resources provide excellent information and opportunities for those interested in pursuing careers in science, particularly in bioinformatics, genomics, and related fields.
