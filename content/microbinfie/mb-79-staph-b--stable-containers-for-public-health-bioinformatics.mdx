# StaPH-B Containers for Public Health Bioinformatics

**Speakers:**
- Dr. Erin Young, Utah Department of Health
- Dr. Kelsey Florek, Wisconsin State Laboratory of Hygiene

In this discussion, Dr. Erin Young and Dr. Kelsey Florek explain the role of StaPH-B containers in public health bioinformatics. The main goal of StaPH-B is to streamline biology processes and make them more accessible to everyone.

For more information and resources, visit the [StaPH-B GitHub repository](https://github.com/StaPH-B).

### Key Points
- **Purpose of StaPH-B Containers:** Simplifies the complex processes of biology through bioinformatics tools.
- **Target Audience:** Public health professionals and anyone interested in making biology more manageable.
- **Outcome:** Easier implementation of bioinformatics in various biology-related projects and research. 

Explore the repository linked above to learn more about the tools and resources available.
