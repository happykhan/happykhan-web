# Episode 125: Kostas Konstantinidis on ANI and Metagenomics

**Podcast:** Microbial Bioinformatics

In this episode, we are thrilled to welcome back Kostas Konstantinidis, a renowned expert in the field of microbiology, to delve deeper into the fascinating topics of Average Nucleotide Identity (ANI) and the field of metagenomics. Here's what you can expect:

## **Key Topics Discussed**

- **Understanding Average Nucleotide Identity (ANI):**
  - Explanation of ANI and its significance in microbial taxonomy.
  - How ANI helps in the classification and identification of microbial species.
  
- **Insights into Metagenomics:**
  - The basics of metagenomics and its transformative impact on microbiology.
  - Techniques and strategies for analyzing complex microbial communities.
  
- **Applications of ANI and Metagenomics:**
  - Real-world applications in environmental and clinical microbiology.
  - How these tools are advancing our understanding of microbial diversity and their roles in different ecosystems.

- **Challenges and Future Directions:**
  - Current challenges in the field and ongoing research efforts to address them.
  - Future prospects for ANI and metagenomics in microbial research.

Join us for an enriching conversation with Kostas Konstantinidis as we explore the cutting-edge techniques and discoveries in microbial bioinformatics.
