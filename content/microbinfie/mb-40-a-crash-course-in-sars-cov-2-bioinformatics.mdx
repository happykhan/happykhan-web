# SARS-CoV-2 Genomics Insights

Over the past year, we've gained significant insights into SARS-CoV-2 genomics. Here, we extract all our insider knowledge to provide honest answers to probing questions from Lee. Our discussion includes:

- **Pipelines for SARS-CoV-2:**
  - Archives & metadata
  - Read filtering
  - Assembly vs. consensus
  - Amplicon data analysis
  - Controls

- **Analysis Insights:**
  - Evaluating if things look too good
  - Coverage considerations
