### Key Points

#### 1. Podcast Origins
- Started in September 2019 as a way to capture informal conversations in microbial bioinformatics
- Originated from a virtual international lab group seminar series
- Chosen as a podcast format to easily share field insights

#### 2. COVID-19 Impact
- Podcast heavily focused on SARS-CoV-2 for approximately two years
- Hosts were professionally redeployed during pandemic
- Continued producing content despite challenging circumstances

#### 3. Future Predictions
- Genomics becoming more trivial and automated
- Potential expansion of genomic diagnostics in medical settings
- Anticipated technological improvements in sequencing read length and quality

### Take-Home Messages
- Podcasts can be an effective medium for sharing specialized knowledge
- Adaptability is crucial in rapidly evolving scientific fields
- Technological advancements are making complex bioinformatics more accessible
