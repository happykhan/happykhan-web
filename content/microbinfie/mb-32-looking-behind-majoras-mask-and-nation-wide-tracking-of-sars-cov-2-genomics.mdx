## Building Systems for the Analysis of SARS-CoV-2 Genomes

Join Nick Loman, Sam Nicholls, and Radosław Popławski as they discuss the development of systems to support the analysis of hundreds of thousands of *SARS-CoV-2* genomes during the pandemic, notably without the use of Excel.

### About the Preprint: MAJORA

Learn more about MAJORA, a system for continuous integration supporting decentralized sequencing for *SARS-CoV-2* genomic surveillance. You can access the preprint [here](https://doi.org/10.1101/2020.10.06.328328).
