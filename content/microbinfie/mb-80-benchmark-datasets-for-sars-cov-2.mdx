# Benchmark Datasets for SARS-CoV-2

In this discussion, we are joined by Lingzi Xiaoli and Jill Hagey to explore their benchmark datasets for SARS-CoV-2.

## Additional Resources

- **SARS-CoV-2 Datasets**: For more information on their SARS-CoV-2 datasets, visit the [CDC GitHub Repository](https://github.com/CDCgov/datasets-sars-cov-2).

- **Previous Research**: You can also check out their previous paper on bacterial datasets at [PeerJ](https://peerj.com/articles/3893/).
