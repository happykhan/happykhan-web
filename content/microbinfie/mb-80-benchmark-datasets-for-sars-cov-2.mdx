## Benchmark Datasets for SARS-CoV-2

We are excited to introduce Lingzi Xiaoli and Jill Hagey, who are joining us to discuss their benchmark datasets for *SARS-CoV-2*. You can find more information on their work by visiting their [GitHub repository](https://github.com/CDCgov/datasets-sars-cov-2).

### Additional Resources

- For details on previous papers related to bacterial datasets, you can refer to [this article](https://peerj.com/articles/3893/).

- **Lingzi Xiaoli** can be connected with on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/lingzi-xiaoli-27b87174/).
