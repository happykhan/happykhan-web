# Comparative Genomics: New Developments

Today, we're discussing some exciting new developments in the field of comparative genomics. We are pleased to have with us two distinguished guests:

- **Dr. Zamin Iqbal**: Research Group Leader at the European Bioinformatics Institute.
- **Dr. Grace Blackwell**: A researcher jointly associated with Dr. Zamin Iqbal's group at the European Bioinformatics Institute and Nick Thomson's team at the Wellcome Sanger Institute.

Stay tuned as we delve into the latest advancements and insights from these experts in comparative genomics.

The speakers on the Microbial Bioinformatics podcast episode include co-hosts Dr. Lee Katz, Dr. Nabil Ali Khan, and Dr. Andrew Page, along with guest speakers Dr. Zaman Iqbal, a research group leader at the European Bioinformatics Institute, and Dr. Grace Blackwell, a postdoctoral fellow working jointly at the European Bioinformatics Institute and the Wellcome Sanger Institute.
