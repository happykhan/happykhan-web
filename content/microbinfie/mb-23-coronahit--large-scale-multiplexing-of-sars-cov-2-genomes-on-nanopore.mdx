# Interview with the Authors of CoronaHiT

We had the opportunity to chat with the creators of CoronaHiT, a groundbreaking tool that enables the sequencing of up to 94 SARS-CoV-2 samples on a single MinION flow cell. This innovation not only reduces the cost of sequencing threefold but also simplifies and accelerates the protocol.

**Authors Featured:**

- Justin O'Grady
- David Baker

**Interviewers:**

- Andrew Page
- Nabil-Fareed Alikhan

## Key Discussion Points

- **How CoronaHiT Works:** The authors delve into the mechanics of CoronaHiT, explaining how it efficiently multiplexes samples for sequencing.
  
- **Development History:** Learn about the inspiration and efforts that went into developing CoronaHiT.
  
- **Impact and Benefits:** Explore why CoronaHiT is vital for current sequencing efforts, especially in light of the COVID-19 pandemic.
