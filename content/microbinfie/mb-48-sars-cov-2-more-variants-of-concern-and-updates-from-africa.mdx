# Latest Developments in SARS-CoV-2 Genomics

### Discussion Overview

In this session, we explore the recent advancements in the genomics of SARS-CoV-2 over the past two weeks. The discussion with Peter van Heusden focuses on the expanding list of Variants of Concern and updates from Africa.

### Key Highlights

- The conversation addressed the dynamics of SARS-CoV-2 variants, including epidemiological and genomic insights.
- Specific attention was given to the African continent's current status and efforts in tracking these variants.
- The studies and resources shared provide a broader view of the continuous research and surveillance necessary to understand and combat COVID-19 effectively. 

This discussion underscores the importance of staying informed on the evolving landscape of COVID-19 variants globally.
