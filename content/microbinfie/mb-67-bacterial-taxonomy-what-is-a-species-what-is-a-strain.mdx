# Navigating Bacterial Taxonomy

In today’s exploration of the intricate world of bacterial taxonomy, we are fortunate to have two esteemed guests guiding us through. Our guests are:

## Dr. Leighton Pritchard

Dr. Leighton Pritchard is a Strathclyde Chancellor's Fellow at the *Strathclyde Institute of Pharmacy and Biomedical Sciences* at the University of Strathclyde.

## Dr. Conor Meehan

Dr. Conor Meehan serves as an assistant professor in *molecular microbiology* at the University of Bradford.

Join us as we delve into the complexities of categorizing and understanding the rich diversity of bacteria!
