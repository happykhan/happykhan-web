![Me](./Nabil-FareedAlikhan-portSQ.jpg "Me")

I am currently [Senior Bioinformatician](https://www.psi.ox.ac.uk/our-team/nabil-fareed-alikhan) at the [Centre for Genomic Pathogen Surveillance, University of Oxford](https://www.pathogensurveillance.net/) working with [David Aanensen](https://www.bdi.ox.ac.uk/Team/david-aanensen). Previously, I served as Interim Head of Informatics at [Quadram Institute Bioscience](https://quadram.ac.uk/), where I originally joined as a Bioinformatics Scientific Programmer working with [Andrew Page](https://www.linkedin.com/in/andrewjpage/). Earlier in my career, I was a Senior Research Fellow with
[Mark Achtman](https://royalsociety.org/people/mark-achtman-10964/) at the [University of Warwick](https://warwick.ac.uk/fac/sci/med/research/biomedical/labs/machtman/achtmanlab/).

I earned my PhD in 2015, under the supervision of [Scott
Beatson](http://www.aidrc.org.au/scott-beatson) at the [University of
Queensland](http://www.aidrc.org.au/), where I also completed dual undergraduate degrees - a
Bachelor of Information Technology and a Bachelor of Science (2008).
